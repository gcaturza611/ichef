Culinary and Commercial Cookery Entrepreneurship

Cookery | Food and Beverage Services

An exciting career in the world of Culinary and Hospitality!
This program provides you with the fundamental knowledge and skills in Culinary Arts. Learn the basic competencies of food preparation and presentation, safety and sanitation, kitchen management and guest services. Prepare international dishes side by side our Masterchefs in our state of the art commercial kitchens to prepare you for an International Culinary career in restaurants, hotels, cruise ships, commercial kitchens or become an entrepreneur and start your own business.

Baking and Patisserie Entrepreneurship

Bread and Pastry | Food and Beverage Services

Be different and become a Professional Pastry Chef!
This course gives you the opportunity to master the skills of Baking and Pastry Production in our modern and industry standard pastry kitchen. Work with our Pastry Masterchef and learn the core competencies of preparing bread and pastry products, decorating techniques, packaging methods, kitchen management and guest services. In the end, be prepared to enter the exciting realm of Pastry and work in international hotels, restaurants, cruise ships, pastry kitchens or operate your own baking and pastry business..
